Short Stories

These stories were written for the r/rational biweekly challenges. The challenges are always centered around a (usually broadly defined) theme, and some of the stories work best when read with the theme in mind. All of these stories are stand-alone.

Immortem - Theme: Pre-Mortem - In a monastic enclave, hidden from the eyes of a ruined world, the last immortal meditates.

The Arahat Signal - Theme: Meditation - A message is hidden in the depths of the human mind.

The Emperor's Final Meditation - Theme: The Mentor - A young Marc Aurel suspects his favourite teacher may hide a dark secret.

Adversaries - Theme: Infighting - A writer and his editor struggle with their work and each other.

Novellas

The Library Unpublished - A bibliophile and a book thief find a book which shouldn't exist. As one mystery leads to another, their journey begins to challenge their understanding of reality.
